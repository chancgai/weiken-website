Corporate Milestone
December 1, 2025

WCAN Amorphous Technology (Zhejiang) Officially Established Through Strategic Restructuring

WCAN Group completes strategic restructuring — WCAN Amorphous Technology (Zhejiang) Co., Ltd. is officially established, inheriting over 15 years of amorphous material R&D and industrialization expertise.

Integrated Platform

As a core subsidiary of WCAN Group (annual revenue exceeding RMB 6 billion), WCAN Amorphous has become the industry's only fully integrated platform: "Amorphous Alloy R&D + High-Quality Strip Manufacturing + Motor Core Mass Production."

Scale and Strength

Annual capacity: 30,000 tons of amorphous ribbon, 20,000 tons of transformer cores. Facility: 31,000 m² in Ningbo Cixi. 44 patents (12 invention). 3 ten-thousand-ton production lines with 3 laser scribing lines.

With deep integration with WCAN Technology (Suzhou) and its 20 years of motor core experience, the new entity aims to become a globally leading iron-based amorphous materials platform.
