WCAN Expands Global Market — New Partnerships in Vietnam and South America

WCAN Amorphous has further expanded its global presence with new client collaborations in Vietnam, South America, and India.

Transformer Core Exports

As a long-term partner to mainstream domestic transformer manufacturers (Yangdian, Gaojing, TBEA, Senyuan), WCAN's ribbon products are widely used. Now products have been exported to Vietnam, South America, India, supporting low-carbon power grid retrofitting worldwide.

Motor Core Global Reach

WCAN Technology provides electrical steel, motor cores, stators/rotors, and amorphous motor cores to LG, Samsung, Hyundai, Kia, BorgWarner, CRRC, Sanhua, Mando and other global leaders.

This expansion reinforces WCAN's position as a truly global amorphous materials platform.
