WCAN Joins National Energy-Saving Technology Promotion Program

WCAN's iron-based amorphous alloy technology has been officially recognized as a national key energy-saving technology, marking a significant policy endorsement.

Environmental Impact

Using WCAN's annual 30,000-ton ribbon capacity: if applied to high-efficiency transformers, manufacturing-phase savings = 20 million kWh (≈ 16,000 tons coal reduction). Over 25-year lifecycle: 4.5 billion kWh saved, 3.6 million tons CO2 reduced.

EHS Excellence

WCAN strictly adheres to ISO 14001 and ISO 45001, with closed-loop water circulation and waste gas treatment. Hierarchical risk control ensures zero-accident operations.

This recognition validates WCAN's commitment to sustainable manufacturing and global decarbonization.
