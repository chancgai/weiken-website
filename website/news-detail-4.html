WCAN-Z Next-Generation Amorphous Ribbon Optimized for NEV Drive Motor Applications

WCAN officially releases WCAN-Z, a next-generation iron-based amorphous ribbon specifically designed for NEV drive motors, with comprehensively optimized electromagnetic and mechanical properties.

Breakthrough Performance

WCAN-Z achieves Bs ≥ 1.65T (vs 1.62T for WCAN-H), with significantly lower core loss across all frequency ranges. At P1.0/400Hz, core loss is ≤ 2.5 W/kg; at P1.0/1000Hz, ≤ 7 W/kg.

Enhanced Mechanical Properties

WCAN-Z features improved toughness and reduced hardness, making it better suited for motor core stamping processes. The lower saturation magnetostriction coefficient (1.7 × 10−6 annealed) reduces operational noise.

This product positions WCAN at the forefront of the rapidly growing NEV amorphous motor market.
