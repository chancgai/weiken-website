WCAN 240mm Wide Amorphous Composite Ribbon Successfully Commissioned

WCAN Amorphous Technology proudly announces that its 240mm wide amorphous composite ribbon has been successfully commissioned, marking a significant milestone.

A New Chapter in Composite Ribbon Technology

The 240mm wide composite ribbon, produced by bonding 5 to 8 layers of iron-based amorphous strips, represents WCAN's latest advancement. With a width of up to 240mm — the widest in the industry — this significantly enhances mass production feasibility.

Available with either no insulation coating or self-bonding coating, compatible with both in-mold and external gluing processes.

Superior Electromagnetic Performance

At P1.0/400Hz, core loss is just 2.5 W/kg — significantly lower than non-oriented silicon steel. At P1.0/2000Hz, it remains competitive at 20 W/kg.

These figures make WCAN composite ribbon ideal for NEV drive motors, smart appliances, drones, and industrial high-speed spindles.

Next-Generation Production Line Under Construction

To meet growing demand, WCAN has initiated a new-generation composite line with higher speeds and wider capability.

With this breakthrough, WCAN Amorphous solidifies its industry leadership, driving the global transition to more efficient motor solutions.
